About Me

My name is Nicholas Campbell, I am 23 years old, and I am currently taking a boot camp for Web Development.

I have worked in retail and a retirement home for the past 4 years. Recently i decided that I don't want to be stuck in these dead end jobs any longer. I looked around for different jobs that i met the requirements for and found nothing interesting.

While in the process of looking for something else to do with my life, my closest friends were taking a bootcamp and following their dreams through it. I saw how successful and happy they were with the program and decided i would go for it as well.

Web Development has always been something i was interested in and I am happy to be in this course trying to make a good change in my life.
